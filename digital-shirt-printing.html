screen and direct printing t shirts on demand with a colorful twist

let’s cut to the chase: you want your customers to wear your full print shirts every day, so you can't afford to have them break down or lose color after a few wears. if you want to avoid wasting time and money on something that won't hold up, apliiq has got you covered with the best t shirt printing quality on the market.

we use nothing but state-of-the-art printing techniques, which are thick and vibrant, to make sure your digital print shirt designs don’t fade or crack when washed. whether you prefer screen printing or dtg printing, we’ve got you covered. having trouble making up your mind?

dtg printed shirts direct to garment uses ink to print directly onto the fibers of the fabric, meaning you don't feel the design or ink when you touch it. meanwhile, with screen printing, a mesh screen is used to separate out areas of the shirt that need to be printed, and then specially mixed inks are pressed through the mesh with a squeegee onto the fabric for a durable print.
